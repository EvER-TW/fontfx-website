FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What features does FontFX support? ▼

The Free plan includes: italic angle, gradient fill, single-layer stroke, basic shadow, outer glow and inner shadow effects, 10 presets, 22 fonts, and PNG 1x output. Premium additionally unlocks: all 9 effects (3D, neon, metallic, long shadow, reflection, bubble, glitch, etc.), dual stroke, arc layout, text plate, custom font upload, 32+ presets, PNG 1x/2x/3x multi-resolution output, and unlimited uses.

Do I need design experience? ▼

Not at all. The Free plan comes with 10 basic presets, and Premium offers 32+ (Xiaohongshu covers, TikTok titles, Douyin styles, etc.) — one click to produce professional results. If you want to fine-tune, every parameter has an intuitive slider.

Will it support future Canva versions? ▼

Yes. FontFX is continuously maintained to ensure compatibility with the latest Canva versions. We build with the official Canva App SDK and follow Canva's API updates.

Can Canva do Chinese italic text? ▼

Canva doesn't natively support Chinese italic. The FontFX plugin lets you tilt Chinese text from 0° to 35° directly inside the Canva canvas, combined with stroke, neon, gradient and other effects — no need to switch to Photoshop or Illustrator.

Can I edit text after adding it to the canvas? ▼

Yes. FontFX uses Canva's App Element API, so text placed on the canvas retains full editing capability — click the element to reopen the app panel, where you can modify the text content, angle, effects, and all other parameters.

How is FontFX different from using Photoshop? ▼

The biggest difference is efficiency and editability. The Photoshop workflow requires: export → open PS → apply effects → save image → paste back into Canva, taking about 5-10 minutes and producing a non-editable image. FontFX works directly inside Canva, finishes in 30 seconds, and placed text remains fully re-editable.

Can I use FontFX output commercially? ▼

Yes. Text effects created with FontFX can be used for any commercial purpose, including social media posts, e-commerce product images, advertisements, YouTube thumbnails, and more. No usage restrictions or additional licensing required.
